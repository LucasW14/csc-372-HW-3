Top Tier Eats

Three spots, three meals each, no regret.

Welcome All

Welcome to Top Tier Eats, where the mission is simple: spotlight the best food spots around UNCG that are absolutely worth your time and appetite. Whether you’re craving savory comfort food, crave-worthy late-night bites, or hidden gems off the main streets, this blog shows you where to go and what to order. I’ve tried countless meals around campus, and I’m sharing the standout places that impressed me the most no filler, just flavor. Each featured restaurant comes with top dish recommendations to make deciding what to eat way easier (and way more delicious). Let’s dig in and explore the best of Greensboro’s eats near UNCG!

The Upper Deck

Location: 948 A Walker Ave, Greensboro, NC 27403
Specialties: Burgers, Fries, American Eatery
Hours: Sun–Wed 11am–12am
Special Hours: 11am–1am

This restaurant is known for their amazing american cuisine that is cooked in less than 15 minutes. They also have an spectacular night time vibe with an upper deck view along with music that plays until midnight allowing you to party amuse yourself throughout the night.

Recommended Dishes

The Sweet Spot

10.99

Seasoned fries layered with mac & cheese, sweet BBQ chicken, drizzled with Deck sauce & ranch. Finishing with bread & butter pickles for a sweet & smokey bite.

Dish image credit: https://www.thefoodinmybeard.com/cheese-fries-mac-and-cheese/

Cheeseburger Bliss

12.99

Seasoned straight fries loaded with two juicy quarter-pound burger patties, creamy mac & cheese. A drizzle of ranch & our house made Deck sauce, all topped with crisp dill pickles. A full blown burger experience - no bun required.

Dish image credit: https://upperdecknc.com

The Melt

9.99

A large grilled quesadilla loaded with melted Monterey jack & cheddar, sauteed peppers, onions & chicken. Served with lettuce shred, fresh Pico & cool sour cream. Classic, cheesy & satisfying every time.

Dish image credit: https://poormansgourmetkitchen.com/quesadilla-with-grilled-chicken-and-jack-cheese.html

East Coast Wings + Grill

Location: 338 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403
Specialties: Wings, sandwiches, fries
Hours: Daily 11am–10pm

Restaurant that specializes in wings giving a variety of different flavors and heat intensities. They allow you to choose a heat index that brings firey sensations to your tongue.

Recommended Dishes

9.99

All flavors are mixed with our national award-winning heat level of your choice. Two wing flavors are included – additional flavors are only 99¢ each.
